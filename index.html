Selection of Features

Custom identity

Treffen offers you an app with a “Tailored Identity” feature, customized based on your event’s needs and identity.

Paperless tickets

Your event is now PAPERLESS! Attendees buy their tickets through the app to have it on their mobile phones all the time.

Live Announcements

You don’t want your attendees to miss anything throughout the event! Keep them up to date with this feature, ALWAYS!

Venue map

No more getting lost in an event while using Treffen! It provides the user with a map of the venue so any place would be accessible.

Networking

You can now access the attendees’ profiles, check their biography and connect/talk with them via chatting rooms on the app!

Analytics and reports

You can measure your success rate by viewing all the reports written about your event, and the analytics provided on the app.

Agenda

No more paper agendas! Treffen provides the attendees with a digital agenda to stay updated with what’s happening now and next.

My Schedule

This feature helps the attendees not waste their time writing down their schedules. They can now construct it on Treffen App!

Sponsors

Sponsors mainly care about showcasing their logo in an event. So, Treffen allows you to post as many sponsors as you want.
